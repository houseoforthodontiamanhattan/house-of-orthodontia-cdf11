How long will my Invisalign treatment take at your Manhattan office?
Most Invisalign treatments at House of Orthodontia last between 12 and 18 months, though minor cases may be completed in as little as 6 months. Are your orthodontists licensed to provide Invisalign in New York State?
Absolutely. All orthodontists at House of Orthodontia are licensed by the State of New York and have completed specialized training in orthodontics, including extensive experience with Invisalign. What is the process for starting Invisalign treatment at House of Orthodontia?
Your Invisalign journey starts with a complimentary consultation at our Manhattan office, where we discuss your goals and take a digital scan of your teeth—no messy impressions needed. We’ll create a 3D treatment plan to show you a preview of your future smile. Once you approve the plan, your custom aligners are made and typically arrive in 2-3 weeks. You’ll receive sets of aligners to change at home every 1-2 weeks, with check-in appointments every 6-8 weeks to monitor your progress.
