Frequently Asked Questions About Self-Ligating Braces

How much do self-ligating braces cost at House of Orthodontia in Manhattan, and do you offer payment plans?

How long does treatment with self-ligating braces usually take at your Manhattan office?
Most of our patients wearing self-ligating braces complete treatment within 12 to 24 months. The exact timeline depends on your specific orthodontic needs and how closely you follow your treatment plan. Self-ligating braces can sometimes reduce overall treatment time compared to traditional braces. At your initial visit, we’ll provide a personalized estimate based on your case.
Are your orthodontists licensed and experienced with self-ligating braces?
Yes, all orthodontists at House of Orthodontia are board-certified and licensed to practice in New York State. Our team has extensive experience with self-ligating braces and stays up-to-date with the latest advancements in orthodontic technology. Dr. Yaniv I. What is the process for getting self-ligating braces at House of Orthodontia in Manhattan?
The process starts with a complimentary consultation at our Manhattan office, where we’ll take digital X-rays and photos to assess your smile. If you’re a candidate for self-ligating braces, we’ll create a custom treatment plan and explain each step. At your next visit, we’ll place the self-ligating brackets and archwires, a process that usually takes about an hour. You’ll then visit us every 6-8 weeks for adjustments. Our team is always available to answer questions and provide care throughout your treatment.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care for self-ligating braces after treatment is complete?
We stand by the quality of our orthodontic care. After your self-ligating braces treatment is complete, we provide retainers and detailed instructions to help you maintain your results. Follow-up visits are scheduled to monitor your teeth and ensure your smile stays aligned. If you experience any issues after your braces are removed, our team is here in Manhattan to assist you with any necessary adjustments or repairs.
